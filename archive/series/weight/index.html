WEIGHT

WEIGHT is a sculptural series about a form under pressure.

The same figure appears in three different states. What changes is not the body, but its ability to hold itself. The material defines the posture and fixes the moment when the form begins to lose stability.

There is no movement. The figure does not resist. It only responds to the force acting on it.

The work does not show the moment of collapse. It shows what remains after. The form is still present, but its strength is no longer the same.

WEIGHT explores the limit between holding and giving in.
