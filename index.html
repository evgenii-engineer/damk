Artist creating handmade art objects

Concrete figures, sculptures and personal objects by EVG DAMK.

My name is DAMK. I make paintings, characters and objects from things I remember — and things I never want to forget.

I grew up in a post-Soviet city where garages, playgrounds, cracked tiles and old cars meant safety.
Now I live in Lisbon and I rebuild those scenes — sometimes in paint, sometimes in plastic, sometimes as a small monkey figure that follows me everywhere.

I work with memory, loneliness, and everyday urban silence.
My pieces live somewhere between street culture and soft sadness.

I want to show a visual world that feels forgotten, but familiar.
The places that shaped us, even if nobody noticed them.

My practice mixes nostalgia, street memory, irony and quiet objects — like playgrounds without kids, or toys that never smile.
